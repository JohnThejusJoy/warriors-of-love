St. John the Baptist

We call him St. John the Baptist. Even in his mother’s womb, he leapt at the presence of Jesus — a divine recognition of the Messiah. Fear never held him, even facing death; his courage and devotion were absolute.

John’s mission was clear: to point everyone to Jesus, to prepare the way for Him. He lived boldly and simply, showing us that true greatness is in serving God and making His name known. Jesus Himself said there was no one greater than John, yet he humbly pointed beyond himself to the One who must increase.

His life reminds us to let go of fear, embrace our mission, and always direct hearts toward Jesus. Leaning on his example helps us live with courage, purpose, and devotion, even when life overwhelms us.
