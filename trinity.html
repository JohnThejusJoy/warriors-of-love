The Holy Trinity – Abba, Jesus, and the Holy Spirit

The Trinity is not a squad, not a team, but the very origin of all love and power. The Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit are not three gods, but one God in a perfect communion of love.

Abba is continuously pouring Himself in love into the Son. The Son is infinitely loving Abba back. That eternal, unstoppable exchange of love is the Holy Spirit — Love Himself, the Fire who moves all creation.

The Trinity is not just a doctrine—it’s the beating heart of reality. - The Father: Source of all. - The Son: Love made flesh. - The Spirit: The fire that fills us.

We’re invited into this divine life every time we pray, every time we receive the sacraments, every time we love. The Trinity is not distant. It’s home.
