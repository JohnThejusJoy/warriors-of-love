Jesus – The Son

Jesus is Love with a face. He is God who got His hands dirty, who walked our dust, who bled for our freedom. He is the Lion of Judah and the Lamb of God.

He didn't come to start a religion; He came to start a Revolution of Love. He broke the chains of death and opened the gates of Heaven. He is the Way, the Truth, and the Life.

In Him, we find our true identity. We are not orphans; we are co-heirs with the King. Follow Him, and you will never walk in darkness.
