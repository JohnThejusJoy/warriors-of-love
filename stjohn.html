St. John the Baptist

We call him St. John the Baptist. He was the fearless prophet who prepared the way for Jesus, living in the wilderness and proclaiming the coming of the Messiah.

He is a model of courage, simplicity, and absolute devotion to God. Leaning on his guidance helps us live boldly for Christ, even when the world seems overwhelming.

His life reminds us to listen to God, live with purpose, and point others toward Jesus.
