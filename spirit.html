Holy Spirit – The Fire

The Holy Spirit is the Wildfire of God. He is the Breath in your lungs and the Fire in your soul. He is the Power that raised Jesus from the dead, and He lives in you.

He is the Advocate, the Comforter, the one who whispers truth when the world screams lies. He turns timid hearts into warriors.

Don't just believe in Him—be filled by Him. Let Him ignite your life with passion, courage, and supernatural love. He is the current in the Powerhouse.
