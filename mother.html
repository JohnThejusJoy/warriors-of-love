Our Mother – The Queen

Mary is the Masterpiece of God. She is the Ark of the New Covenant, the one who carried the Fire of God within her. Her "Yes" changed history forever.

She isn't just a statue; she is a Warrior Queen. She crushes the serpent's head and leads the army of angels. When you are weak, run to her. She is the Mother of the Church and your Mother.

To love Jesus is to love the one He loved most. She will always, always lead you straight to her Son. "Do whatever He tells you."
