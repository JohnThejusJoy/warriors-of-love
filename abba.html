Abba – The Father

Abba is the Source. The Beginning. The One who dreamt you into existence before the stars were lit. He isn't a distant clockmaker; He is the Father who runs to meet you.

His love is the foundation of the universe. Secure, unshakeable, and fiercely protective. In a world of chaos, He is the Rock.

Jesus came to reveal Him. Every miracle, every word was to show us the face of the Father. To know Abba is to know you are wanted, you are held, and you are loved infinitely.
